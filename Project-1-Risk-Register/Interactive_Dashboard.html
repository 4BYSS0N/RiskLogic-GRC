Risk Assessment Executive Summary

As part of our strategic roadmap for ShopLogic India, we have conducted a comprehensive risk assessment utilizing the NIST SP 800-30 Revision 1 framework. This assessment identifies critical vulnerabilities within our digital infrastructure, ranging from Customer PII databases to cloud storage configurations.

The analysis reveals a high inherent risk posture due to rapid digital expansion. However, through targeted mitigation strategies—specifically focusing on Identity Access Management (MFA) and Data Encryption—we project a significant reduction in our residual risk profile, aligning with the DPDP Act 2023 compliance mandates.
